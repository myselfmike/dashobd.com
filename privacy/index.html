DashUI OBD-II Privacy Agreement

Last updated: May 22, 2026

This Privacy Agreement explains how DashUI OBD-II handles information when you use the DashUI app, website, and related support channels. This page is intended as a plain-language starting policy and should be reviewed before final app-store submission.

Information the app may use

DashUI OBD-II may read vehicle diagnostic information through an OBD-II adapter so the app can display live vehicle data, diagnostic trouble codes, connection status, and related app features. This may include values such as sensor readings, emissions-related codes, adapter details, and connection logs.

Information you choose to provide

If you contact DashUI for support, feedback, testing, or business questions, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, screenshots, diagnostic logs, device details, vehicle details, or other information needed to understand your request.

How information is used

  • To provide OBD-II diagnostics, live data, and app functionality.
  • To troubleshoot crashes, connection problems, adapter issues, or vehicle-data display problems.
  • To respond to support requests and improve DashUI.
  • To meet legal, security, app-store, or fraud-prevention requirements.

Data sharing

DashUI does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the app or website, respond to a support request, comply with legal obligations, protect users, or work with service providers who help provide hosting, analytics, crash reporting, email, or support services.

Vehicle data

Vehicle diagnostic data can reveal information about a vehicle’s status, performance, faults, or usage. Do not send diagnostic logs or screenshots to DashUI unless you are comfortable sharing that information for support or troubleshooting.

Children’s privacy

DashUI is not intended for children under 13. DashUI does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your choices

You may contact DashUI to request access, correction, or deletion of information you previously provided through support or feedback channels, subject to legal and operational limits.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact DashUI at dashuiobdii@gmail.com.
