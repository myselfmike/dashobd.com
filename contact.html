What to include

Better details help us improve diagnostics, adapter compatibility, and future DashUI features.

  • Vehicle: year, make, model, engine, and trim if known.
  • Adapter: brand, model, connection type, and where it was purchased.
  • Device: phone/tablet model, Android version, and DashUI app version.
  • Issue: what happened, what you expected, and whether it repeats.
  • Datalogs: attach only logs you are comfortable sharing.
